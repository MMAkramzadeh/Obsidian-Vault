## The Non-Negotiables :
> **The body remembers consequences better than words.**

### First Non-Negotiable:
Waking up at Same time everyday. Not-Negotiable. Start from 10:00 am and slowly Turning it to 9:00.
Stand up, Do some exercise, Some cold Water and that's it. Day Starts!

***Consequences?*** No Optional Dopamine that day :) 
### Second Non-Negotiable:
Sleep at the same time each night also. Every night, around 1:30 you go to bed. You will just lay down and do nothing till you sleep.
No Screens and other shit. you teach your body that when the time comes, you are in the bed not sleep.

***Consequences?*** Caffeine intake is forbidden. None! Deal with it.
### Third Non-Negotiable:
60mins of just Maximum focus to Python or your uni stuff. It can be more but it can never be less. Put in the time so that after some time, the time itself becomes money.

***Consequences?*** The Next Day the block becomes 90mins
### Fourth Non-Negotiable:
10mins of full-body movement each day. Sit-ups, Squats and Push-ups. Thats it!

***Consequences?*** Cold Shower or ice face plunge.
